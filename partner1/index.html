How to Acquire an Enrollment Verification and Advising Transcript from Penn State

Knowing how to acquire an Enrollment Verification (EV) and Advising Transcript from LionPATH is useful to many students who attend Penn State

Many students may need to provide loan servicers EV's or they may even be part of scholarship applications. Students may also need unofficial copies of their transcripts as well which can be requested via Advising Transcript.

  • Enrollment Verification is great way to have quick access to an Enrollment Verification if the document is not needed on offical University letterhead.
  • Advising Transcript is great for studnets to acquire an official copy of their transcript to see a full report of their coursework, grades, earned, and GPA.
